# DataFox Marketing Plan

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days 1-7)

### Build Audience
1. **Twitter/X Building**
   - Create @DataFoxApp account
   - Follow 100 sales influencers daily
   - Share build progress daily
   - Use hashtags: #sales #B2B #startup #funding

2. **LinkedIn Presence**
   - Create company page
   - Connect with 50 sales leaders daily
   - Share insights on sales intelligence

3. **Email List**
   - Build landing page with waitlist
   - Offer early access discount (50% off first year)
   - Target: 100 email signups pre-launch

### Content Creation
1. **Blog Posts**
   - "How to identify companies that just raised funding"
   - "The 3 signals every B2B salesperson should track"
   - "ZoomInfo vs DataFox: 90% cheaper alternative"

2. **Social Content**
   - Daily build updates
   - Screenshots of progress
   - Polls about sales pain points

## Phase 2: Launch (Day 8)

### Launch Strategy
1. **r/sales Launch** (500K+ members)
   - Title: "I built a ZoomInfo alternative for $99/month - tracks funding, hiring, expansion"
   - Content: Show product, pricing, use cases
   - Offer: First 50 get lifetime 20% discount

2. **Indie Hackers** (100K+ members)
   - Share full build story
   - Technical details
   - Revenue goals

3. **Twitter/X Launch**
   - Thread: "I rebuilt DataFox in 4 days"
   - Tag sales influencers
   - Run giveaway: 1 year free for best signal story

4. **Email Launch**
   - Send to waitlist
   - Limited time offer: $299/year (normally $399)
   - Personal onboarding offer

### Launch Goals
- **Signups**: 100 in first week
- **Paying customers**: 10 in first week
- **MRR**: $250 from first week

## Phase 3: Growth (Weeks 2-4)

2. **Video Content**

3. **Guest Posts**
   - Sales blogs: Close.com blog, Outreach blog

1. **Slack Community**

2. **Referral Program**

   - Budget: $500/month to start

2. **Twitter/X Ads**
   - Target followers of sales influencers
   - Interest: Sales, B2B, startups
   - Budget: $300/month

3. **Content Syndication**
   - Republish on Medium, Dev.to
   - LinkedIn articles
   - Sales newsletters sponsorship

### SEO Strategy

2. **Content Calendar**
